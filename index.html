September 2024 - A Review of Brookton’s Development

It’s been a month since the OMERTA Airdrop officially went live... and it’s safe to say that Orbit Cosmos devs have done A LOT throughout the past thirty days or so!

Let’s take a look at what went down…

  • OMERTA Full Game Cinematic Trailer: The ongoing creation of 5 distinct Brookton environments that will be used in the Full Game trailer (including a bullet train that will also be playable in the game.
  • Character Creation BTS Access: We were given a glimpse into OMERTA’s character creation process for the Full Game trailer. To date, over 35 human and creature characters have been finalized.
  • Unreal Engine Chaos Cloth Experiments: The intricacies of in game physics were tested through CFX and simulations using Unreal Engine’s latest Chaos Physics, including walking, running, sprinting, jumping, dodging, and gun shooting, as well as vehicles such as cars (and car destruction, bikes, trains, helicopters, and more.
  • Concrete Jungle Construction: Devs continue designing Brookton’s architectural character, including buildings, bridges, and other architectural features that prove vital in making the city a living, breathing metropolis.
  • From Paper to Unreal Engine: 3 hand sketched special cars transformed into functionally rigged and textured 3D vehicles. This process includes a dynamic destruction system that allows in game damage and destruction to take place in real time and in accordance with impact intensities.
  • AAA Website For a AAA Game: Wireframes for the new AAA standard OMERTA website established, facilitating an even smoother Airdrop mission experience.
